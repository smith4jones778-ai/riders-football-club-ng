Our History

Riders FC was founded with a vision to redefine passion-driven football. Over the years, we have grown into a competitive and unified football team known for discipline, teamwork, and a relentless fighting spirit. The club has achieved remarkable success at regional and national levels, building a legacy that inspires the next generation of athletes.

Mission & Values

Our Mission: To develop talented footballers through discipline, structured training, and a winning mentality while inspiring the community through excellence on and off the pitch.


  • Teamwork — We move as one.
  • Discipline — The backbone of our success.
  • Dedication — Consistent effort, every single day.
  • Respect — For teammates, fans, opponents, and the game.
  • Growth — We learn, adapt, and evolve.
